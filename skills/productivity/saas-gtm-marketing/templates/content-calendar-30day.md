## Posting Schedule

### X/Twitter: 2 posts/day
- **8:00 AM**: Thread, tip, carousel, or long-form value
- **1:00 PM**: Meme, engagement prompt, social proof, or quick win

### LinkedIn: 1 post/day (Mon-Fri)
- **9:00 AM**: Long-form story, carousel, product demo, testimonial, or hot take
- Weekends: Rest or repurpose top performer

## Automation Setup

**Recommended tools:**
- **Typefully** (X scheduling, threads, analytics)
- **Taplio** (LinkedIn scheduling, carousel maker)
- **Make/Zapier** (cross-post, RSS → social)

**Weekly workflow:**
1. Sunday: Batch-write all posts for the week
2. Monday: Schedule in Typefully + Taplio
3. Daily: 30 min engagement (replies, likes, follows)
4. Friday: Review analytics, note top performers
5. Saturday: Plan next week's content based on what worked
